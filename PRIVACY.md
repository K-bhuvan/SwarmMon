# Privacy Policy

SwarmMon collects operational telemetry from simulated or real robot fleets as
configured by the deploying organization.

## Data Collected

By default, SwarmMon monitors metadata and signal freshness (topics, nodes,
diagnostics, heartbeats). Raw high-volume sensor payloads (camera frames, maps)
are disabled by default.

## Data Storage

Event data is stored locally in the deploying organization's infrastructure
(SQLite for MVP, PostgreSQL in later deployments).

## Responsibility

Deploying organizations are responsible for configuring data retention, access
controls, and compliance with applicable privacy regulations.
